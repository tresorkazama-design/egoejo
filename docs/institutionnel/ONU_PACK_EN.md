## üìã EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

EGOEJO is a digital subsistence infrastructure that connects citizens to social projects with high impact for living systems. The model is based on a **dual economic structure** : a relational economy (SAKA) and an instrumental economy (EUR), strictly separated.

### 4P Model (People, Planet, Purpose, Prosperity)

EGOEJO operates according to a **4P model** aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) :

- **People** : Connect citizens to social projects
- **Planet** : Promote projects with environmental impact
- **Purpose** : Clear and non-negotiable mission (Mission-driven Company)
- **Prosperity** : Subsistence, not accumulation
